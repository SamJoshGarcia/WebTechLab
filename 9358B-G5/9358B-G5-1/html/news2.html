Betag's Approach Towards Tolerance

January 26, 2018

Religious tolerance is an act of accepting everyone's faith, beliefs and lifestyle without any added discrimination. Intolerance of others may lead to bullying, misunderstandings and even violence. Further more, tolerance can be hard to achieve with the existing differences between religions. In the barangay, officials aim to provide the residents with utmost quality service. Ensuring that every one would be safe, living happily and free from harm. The goals of the barangay and tolerance is somewhat the same, because of this, the barangay has set up an organization namely U.B.A.S.

U.B.A.S. stands for "Ugnayan ng Barangay at Simbahan". The organization does not only have a connection with one of the churches located in the area, but all 4. This includes, Episcopal Church of the Epiphany(Anglican), Jesus is Lord Church(Roman Catholic), Iglesia Ni Cristo Lokal Ng La Trinidad (Iglesia ni Cristo), Valley Christian Church(Christian). Different religions,one organization. Officials are starting to introduce events and meetings with all four churches involved. They are taking a step forward towards religious tolerance and bring people more close to one another. And help them understand and respect each and everyone's different perspectives.
