GAD’s Plan: What is it and Who is it for

Brg. Betag, La Trinidad - GAD’s Plan or Gender and Development Plan is a newly implemented plan for all the people of Betag. It aims to make all the people to be productive. The plan is set for the year 2018. Funds were already collected to make this plan feasible.

The plan is masterfully thought of by the Betag Invigorated, an advocate for gender equality. It was first pitched on June 2017 and meetings were preceded. Both the local government and Betag Invigorated planned together and approved it to be set in motion this 2018.

GAD’s plan includes all people of all ages, gender and race. Diversity is the key presented by the plan. It aims to develop projects and programs for the purpose of connecting and ensuring that productivity and equality exists on both genders.

For this year’s first meeting for GAD’s plan, any and everyone interested may go to the BSU gym at Betag, La Trinidad at 1:00 in the afternoon this Friday, February 2, 2017. The programs and projects that will be implemented in the plan will be discussed. Also, there will be free snacks so don’t miss out.
