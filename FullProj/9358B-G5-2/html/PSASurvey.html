Betag, La Trinidad - As of survey of the PSA or the Philippine Statistics Authority to Barangay Betag of La Trinidad with the coordination of the past documents of NSO or National Statistics Office, Benguet, the highest number with relation to the total population are females in the age groups 20-24 with 8.07% while the males belonging to age groups 20-24 only have 5.95%. Literacy rate is on high time to Betag, with 98.66% with 3,098 individuals who are 10 years old and above is comprised of 1,432 males and 1,666 females. Forty-one people were accounted as illiterate, 20 of them were males while 21 were females.

There were a total of 783 who were able to graduate from college by which 308 were males and 475 were females. Four males and 3 females were able to obtain units in masters and/or doctorates while sixteen people, 6 males and 10 males, were able to obtain masters and/or doctor's degree.

Seventy-four people were accounted for as solo parents wherein 55 females dominated over 19 males. The main reason for being such was due to death of spouse. This accounted to 31 individuals with 5 males and 26 females representing 41.89% of the total number of solo parents. Followed by spouse's abandonment wherein there were 8 males and 17 females out of the total 25 people accounted for. Solo parenting due to non-marriage was also accounted for wherein 6 were males and 7 were females.

2,727 were accounted for regarding potential labor force which comprised of 1,276 males and 1,451 females. The present labor force includes 961 males and 703 females out of the total 1,664 persons with 961 males and 703 females. This statistic excludes those who are unable, not available and not looking for work. Betag has a high employment rate of 94.77% wherein eighty-seven were not employed. 199 professionals were accounted for wherein 133 males and 66 females.
