Brg. Betag, La Trinidad - Protection Order is a project of our advocacy aimed at providing security as well as services to battered wives. It aims to provide care as well as protection to the battered wife as well as any other affected person close to her. It is planned and negotiated as well coordinated by both the advocacy and the local government of Betag.

The project was first pitched last November 23, 2017 by Betag Invigorated. The proceedings of multiple local meetings then started on November 28, 2017. The project was then pitched to the local government on December 1, 2017 for support. For now, the project is reaching its affirmation with only the approval of both the advocacy’s Executive Director and the Barangay Kagawad’s are needed.

The benefits for the people concerned will include:

1. Security - The Barangay Kagawads will serve as bodyguards.

2. Medical Care - Medical care will be free of charge.

3. Food and Care - Food and grocery items will be provided.

4. Cash Grant - This is still being discussed by the higher-ups.

The Protection Order will be given according to the set days, by which the maximum is 15 days.
