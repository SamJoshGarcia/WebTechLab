Betag Invigorated is the culmination of people united by one cause: Gender Equality.
We are a group of diverse people, no matter the race, dialect, or gender, everyone can be a part of the cause. Currently, there are 156 members of the Betag Community joined in our advocacy and about 12 projects have been deployed throughout the 8 years since the formation. We fight for one cause rooted for the many and that is to fight for equality.
Mission
To encourage and fight for the right of women as well as preserve the ties between gender and ensure that everyone is equal.
Vision
We envision a community that is diverse, equal and respectful towards one another no matter the gender.
