About Us

Enlightenment of Betag Humanism is about a group of college students who provide human rights news, events and other activities that inform, assist and unite individuals, society, and organizations in Barangay Betag. We raise awareness about the rights of people within the barangay. We scrupulously investigate abuses, expose the facts widely, and pressure those with power to respect rights and secure justice. A Also, we want to have a progressive community where residents are provided with their basic needs, security and good governance in order to fulfill the rights of everyone.
