Who Are We?

Founded in 2018, Open Doors is a team
consisting a total of 7 members based in Betag,
La Trinidad. We aim to build a society where your
religious beliefs, coinciding your lifestyle would not
hinder socialization and respect from other residents

We believe that religion is still a very important part of people's lives, evident in Betag. We work collectively with the barangay towards a similar goal to promote peace, respect and just in the area where such discrimination is voided.

Our Vision

"Tolerance does not mean
tolerating intolerance of others"

Creating a barangay community that overcomes separation and differences due to colliding faiths and beliefs. Envisioning the residents of Betag living in a place where there would be little to no discrimination between different religions and respecting each others shared humanity.

"Accepting others begins with accepting one's self"
