"It's all about mindsets"

January 26, 2018

Given the widespread of varying religious background present in this barangay, it is highly important to advance a “pluralistic mindset with clarity and confidence,” Nestor says. Such mindset cannot be taught overnight to our children; it requires a gradual and constant nurturing of young minds to ensure that when they grow up, they will carry with them the values of love, respect, confidence and altruistic service to humanity, not just to groups with certain beliefs. The bottom-up approach to world problems should start at home and should be shown in their community.
