Strawberry Festival
A grand festive in La trinidad, Benguet that showcase their very own product, Strawberry.
It became a tradition to celebrate this festive yearly. Stalls, activities and contest are held
for about 5 days. Year 2004, they baked a giant strawberr cake that about 1.5 meters high, 2.4 meters
long and weighs about 2.5 tons can produce about 16,000 slices. Hence, it was entitled by the Guinness
World Records as largest fruit shortcake in the world.

ADIVAY Festival It is of the most famous festival in La Trinidad that is being held every month of November
ADIVAY is an ibaloi word meaning "coming together and having fun". This is more on a celebration
of all tribe in La trinidad wherein they create history, share culture and trade products in different tribe.
Concert for a Cause
This is a very famous and inspiring event in each betag, wherein they organize a concert
to help deceased people of betag.

Holy Week(Lucky Summer Visitors)

All religious organization in the entire barangay of betag is invited to join the celebration of hold week
wherein a bunch of fun activities regarding holy week will be done and ceremonies. Thus, they're not obliged

UBAS Anniversary

The Ugnayan ng Barangayarangay at Simbahan or also know as UBAS is an organization of all religions in betag.
Every year, they conduct an anniversary party for the organization where all member in each religion are
invited for a simple event and simple dinner as well.
